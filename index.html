ARL:UT

Applied Research Laboratories at the University of Texas Austin J.J. Pickle Center

Apprentice/Research Assistant

ARL:UT is a University-Affiliated Research Center (UARC) partnered with the University of Texas at Austin. It recieves funding from and contracts with the US Navy. I worked in the Signal and Informational Sciences Laboratory (SISL) in the Geospatial Laser Applications & Measurements (GLAM) group with NASA.
We focused on glacial LiDAR imaging, data visualization, and fluid dynamics. By the end of my apprenticeship, my partner and I wrote a paper about calculating the turbulence of a glacial plume from its satellite LiDAR signature.
